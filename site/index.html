Built for conservation workflows

Everything a conservator needs, from first inspection to final report.

📋

Condition Reports

Structured checklists, controlled vocabulary, damage rating, and professional PDF export. Replace Word templates forever.

🖊️

Image Annotation

Mark cracks, paint loss, corrosion, and other damage directly on photos. Each annotation links to type, severity, and notes.

📄

Treatment Proposals

Methodology, materials, cost estimates, and step-by-step treatment plans. Track progress from proposal to completion.

📁

Project Management

Organize objects, clients, and reports into projects. Track status, timelines, and budgets in one place.

📱

Offline-First

Work in churches, basements, excavation sites, or anywhere without signal. Everything syncs when you reconnect.

🌍

Multilingual Export

Generate reports in Greek, English, and more. Perfect for EU projects, foreign collectors, and international collaborations.

Works where you work

iOS

Native SwiftUI app. Camera capture, on-site annotation, offline storage. Built for iPhone and iPad.

Android

Jetpack Compose with Material 3. Same powerful features, native Android experience.

Web

Desktop companion for report editing, project dashboards, and client management. No install needed.

Who is Conservatio for?

From solo conservators to small museums, we fill the gap between Word files and enterprise systems.

🎨

Private Conservators

Fast condition reports, photo annotation, client management, and professional PDF export. Look more professional with less admin.

🏛️

Small Museums

Affordable collection and conservation management. Object inventory, condition history, and maintenance tracking.

⛪

Churches and Heritage Sites

Simple inventory, photo-based records, maintenance calendar, and restoration history for religious and public heritage.
